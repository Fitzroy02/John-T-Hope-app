# Brand Voice Guide

## Core Lines
**Tagline:** "Our honesty is integrity you can trust."  
**Positioning Line:** "The secure choice for creative and cultural transactions."

---

## Voice Essence
Honesty → Integrity → Trust → Protection → Culture

---

## Tone Pillars

### Honesty
Plain, clear, transparent language.

### Integrity
Every sentence feels intentional and principled.

### Trust
Calm, steady, reliable tone.

### Protection
Safety is implied through structure, not hype.

### Culture
Respect for creative labour and regional identity.

---

## Voice Do's
- Speak with clarity and dignity
- Use clean, declarative sentences
- Honour the user's intelligence
- Keep emotional temperature warm but steady

## Voice Don'ts
- No hype language
- No corporate clichés
- No infantilising tone
- No chaotic phrasing

---

## Example Voice
"We protect the agreements that keep culture alive."

---

**Our voice is our integrity. Our words are our values.**
