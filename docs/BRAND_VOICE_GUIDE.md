# Brand Voice Guide

**Core Taglines:**
- Primary: "Our honesty is integrity you can trust."
- Secondary: "The secure choice for creative and cultural transactions."

---

## Brand Essence

**Honesty → Integrity → Trust → Protection → Culture**

Your voice is:
- **Adult**
- **Clear**
- **Ethical**
- **Culturally literate**
- **Calm and confident**
- **Never hype, never chaos**

Your platform is not a marketplace.
It is a **cultural infrastructure** built on dignity and clarity.

---

## Tone Pillars

### 1. Honesty
Speak plainly.
No jargon.
No manipulation.
No over-promising.

**Voice example:**
"We tell the truth, even when it's simple."

---

### 2. Integrity
Every sentence should feel intentional, grounded, and principled.

**Voice example:**
"We honour agreements and the people who make them."

---

### 3. Trust
Your language should feel like a handshake — steady, adult, reliable.

**Voice example:**
"You can rely on the structure beneath every transaction."

---

### 4. Protection
You don't shout about safety.
You embody it.

**Voice example:**
"Every agreement is supported from the moment it's made."

---

### 5. Culture
You speak with respect for craft, region, and community.

**Voice example:**
"We protect the work that keeps culture alive."

---

## Voice Do's

- Speak with calm authority
- Use clean, declarative sentences
- Honour the user's intelligence
- Centre culture and professionalism
- Keep the emotional temperature warm but steady

## Voice Don'ts

- No hype language ("revolutionary", "game-changing")
- No corporate clichés
- No infantilising tone
- No chaotic or slang-heavy phrasing
- No fear-based messaging
