### Who Pays the Fee?
The fee is paid by the **buyer/client**, not the creative.

### What Is This Fee?
This is a **platform service fee**, not an insurance premium.

### What's Included?
Platform Protection is **automatically included** in all eligible transactions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why do you charge a fee?
The Platform Service Fee funds the infrastructure, protection, and trust systems that make safe cultural transactions possible.

### Is this an insurance premium?
No. This is a platform service fee. We use part of it to purchase group insurance coverage that protects all eligible transactions.

### Can I opt out of Platform Protection?
No. Protection is automatically included to ensure a safe ecosystem for everyone.

### Do creatives pay the fee?
No. The fee is paid by the buyer/client. Creatives receive the full agreed amount.

### Are there any other charges?
No hidden fees. The Platform Service Fee is clearly displayed before you confirm any transaction.
