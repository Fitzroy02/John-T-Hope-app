# Platform Service Fee Structure

The Platform Service Fee funds:
- Platform operations
- Contract tools
- Verification systems
- Platform Protection (via third-party insurer)

It is not an insurance premium.

---

## Fee Table

| Transaction Type | Fee Structure | Covers |
|------------------|---------------|--------|
| Standard Contract | 7% + £1.50 | Platform Protection, operations, contract tools |
| High-Value (£1,000+) | 5% + £2.00 | Same as above, scaled for larger agreements |
| Micro-Transactions (<£50) | 10% flat | Ensures protection remains viable |
| Subscription Services | Included | Protection applies to all eligible transactions |

---

## Notes
- Fee is paid by the **buyer/client**, not the creative.
- Protection is included automatically.
- The platform is not an insurer.
