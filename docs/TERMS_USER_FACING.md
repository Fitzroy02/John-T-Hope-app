## Platform Protection

All eligible transactions on the platform include **Platform Protection**, a service provided through a third‑party insurer. Platform Protection offers support if a contractual agreement cannot be resolved between the parties.

### What Platform Protection Includes

- Access to dispute‑resolution support  
- Coverage of solicitor's fees for eligible claims  
- Claims handled directly by the insurer  

### What the Platform Does Not Do

- The platform is **not** an insurer  
- The platform does **not** provide insurance advice  
- The platform does **not** guarantee outcomes  
- The platform does **not** intervene in legal decisions  

### How It Works

Platform Protection is funded through the Platform Service Fee.  
The platform purchases a group policy from a regulated insurer.  
Users benefit automatically when they transact through the platform.

## Your Responsibilities

As a user of this platform, you agree to:

1. **Provide Accurate Information**: All profile and transaction details must be truthful
2. **Honor Agreements**: Contracts created on the platform are legally binding
3. **Communicate Professionally**: Disputes should be raised in good faith
4. **Respect the Process**: Follow dispute resolution procedures if issues arise

---

## Platform's Responsibilities

The platform commits to:

1. **Maintain Security**: Protect user data and transaction integrity
2. **Provide Tools**: Offer contract creation and management features
3. **Facilitate Protection**: Ensure Platform Protection is applied to eligible transactions
4. **Stay Neutral**: The platform does not take sides in disputes

---

## Legal Boundaries

- Platform Protection is insurance provided by a regulated third party, not by the platform itself
- The platform acts as a facilitator, not as an insurer or legal advisor
- Users are encouraged to seek independent legal advice for complex agreements
- The insurer handles all claims and legal coverage decisions
