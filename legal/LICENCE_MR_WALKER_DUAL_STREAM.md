## 1. Purpose of Agreement

This agreement governs the use of the fictional character **Mr Walker**, owned exclusively by the Licensor, within the Hopenism digital platform for symbolic, educational, and narrative‑adjacent purposes.

---

## 2. Grant of Rights

The Licensor grants the Licensee a **non‑exclusive, non‑transferable, revocable licence** to:

- display Mr Walker's likeness  
- reference Mr Walker in text, audio, or symbolic content  
- include Mr Walker in educational modules  
- use Mr Walker in animations or guided experiences  

This licence does **not** grant ownership, derivative rights, or commercial exploitation rights.

---

## 3. Dual‑Stream Compensation Structure

### 3.1 Appearance Licence Fee

The Licensee shall pay the Licensor a **discounted annual appearance fee** for the right to include Mr Walker within the Hopenism platform.

- Standard IP Licence Value: £X  
- Discounted CIC Rate: £Y  
- Billing Cycle: Annual  

This fee covers the right to display or reference Mr Walker in any form.

### 3.2 Performance‑Based Royalty

The Licensee shall pay the Licensor a royalty based on user interactions involving Mr Walker.

**Royalty Formula:**

```
Royalty = Total Interactions × Rate per Interaction
```

Interaction types include:

- completed modules  
- viewed animations  
- audio playback  
- narrative sequences  
- character‑guided exercises  

The rate per interaction shall mirror the artist streaming rate used within the Hopenism platform.

## 4. Activation Clause

This licence becomes active only when:

- Mr Walker appears in the Hopenism app in any form  
- His likeness, voice, or narrative presence is introduced  
- Any module or animation featuring him goes live  

Before activation, no fees or royalties are due.
