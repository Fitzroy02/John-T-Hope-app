# Hopenism Governance Overview

## IP Ownership Foundation
**Fitzroy B. Edwards**, writing as **John T. Hope**, is the sole creator and intellectual property owner of the character **Mr Walker**.
Hopenism CIC uses Mr Walker under a limited, non-exclusive licence granted by the IP owner.

---

## 1. Purpose
This governance framework ensures that Hopenism CIC operates with:

- ethical integrity
- legal clarity
- civic neutrality
- transparent accountability
- respect for intellectual property boundaries

---

## 2. Separation of Domains

### John T. Hope (Author/Creator)
- Personal creative brand
- IP ownership hub
- Media development center
- Independent author platform

### Hopenism CIC (Licensee)
- Community Interest Company
- Educational platform
- Separate legal entity
- Mission-driven organization

This separation protects both brands and ensures clear ownership.

---

## 3. Governance Structure

### CIC Board
Responsible for:
- Strategic oversight
- Financial stewardship
- Safeguarding
- Regulatory compliance

### Governance Lead
Responsible for:
- Policy enforcement
- IP compliance
- Content review
- Ethical alignment

### Technical Team
Responsible for:
- App development
- Analytics
- Feature implementation
- Stability and security

### IP Owner (Fitzroy B. Edwards / John T. Hope)
Responsible for:
- Character integrity
- Narrative consistency
- Approval of all Mr Walker content
